Company

A Houston-based energy holding company.

Since 1919

Over a century of continuous oil production in Patagonia.

IPP Ltd holds and manages hydrocarbon concessions in Argentina through its operating subsidiary IPP Oil & Gas. Our Alberto and Don Ernesto fields, located in the Golfo San Jorge Basin near Comodoro Rivadavia, Chubut, have been in continuous production since 1919 — making them among the longest-running oil operations in Argentina.
